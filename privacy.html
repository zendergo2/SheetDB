SheetsDB

A Drive App to export a Google Spreadsheet into many different formats.

Privacy Policy

This Policy was last modified on August 10, 2019.

SheetsDB collects and stores only the minimum information it needs to function properly. SheetsDB may load external resources such as jQuery to help it function properly. SheetsDB requires that it communicate between your browser and Google's servers in order to function. Other than the exception(s) above, SheetsDB does not upload, share, or otherwise transfer your data anywhere.

SheetsDB can save and retrieve settings for each Sheet you use it with. These settings may contain information on the structure of your spreadsheets, such as the sheet's identifier or column numbers. No content from your spreadsheets is collected or saved anywhere. I do not have access to any of your settings. SheetsDB stores these settings with Google and nowhere else. SheetsDB briefly processes the content of your spreadsheet in order to generate an export, but none of that information is stored.

I have access only to user statistics, feedback, and errors provided by Google to every developer of Google Sheets Add Ons. I do not have Google Analytics enabled for SheetsDB.
